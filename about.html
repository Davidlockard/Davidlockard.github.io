Hello! I am currently I just completed being a research fellow at New York University’s ITP (Interactive Telecommunication Program), where I earned my masters degree. I concentrated on developing tools for playful learning, and spent much of my time assisting students with their work and looking for new ways to encourage the enjoyment of improvisation, structure, and craft.

I serve as managing editor of Adjacent, ITP's online journal of emerging media.

Previously, I studied screen-based arts at the Bezalel Academy of Arts and Design in Jerusalem, where I’m from. I spent several years producing news journalism for Haaretz and working as a videographer and in theatre. I have worked for several Israeli human rights NGOs and grassroots organizations in education, media, and organizing.

If you like my work, or have any questions or comments, I would love to hear from you at drl349@nyu.edu. Or please get in touch for a resume/CV.
