Hello! I am currently just stopped being a research fellow at New York University’s ITP (Interactive Telecommunication Program), where I earned my masters degree, concentrating on developing tools for playful learning.

I serve as the managing editor of Adjacent, ITP's journal of emerging media, and I spend much of my time assisting students with their work and looking for ways to encourage the enjoyment of improvisation, structure, and craft.

Previously-- I studied screen-based arts at the Bezalel Academy of Arts and Design in Jerusalem, where I’m from. I spent several years producing news journalism for Haaretz and working as a videographer and in theatre. Also, I have worked for several Israeli human rights NGOs and grassroots organizations in education, media, and organizing.

If you like my work, or have any questions or comments, I would love to hear from you at drl349@nyu.edu. Or please get in touch for a full CV.
