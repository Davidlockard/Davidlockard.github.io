Adjacent

Adjacent is ITP's online journal, which I helped start along with a wonderful team of students, under the direction of faculty member Nancy Hechinger. The journal's mission is to function as a scholarly stage for the department community (students, faculty, alumni) for sharing research, essays, and other types of writing.

I have served as managing editor since the publication's inception. We have published four issues to date, on a biannual basis. In November 2018 we held our first conference, to celebrate our 4th issue.
