Adjacent is ITP's new online journal, which I helped found, along with a wonderful team of students. Its mission is to share research, reflection, observations, and opinions from and for the diverse creators that are exploring the possibilities and directions at the frontiers of media and technology.

Since its inception, under the direction of Nancy Hechinger, I have served as managing editor. So far we have published three issues, one per semester.
