Workshop at Bauhaus 100-year anniversary


Along with Mathura Govindarajan, will give a lecture and workshop at Bauhaus Dessau foundation's School Fundamental. This festival is part of the centenary celebrations for the founding of the Bauhaus School. We will be talking about contemporary design education, and more specifically about approaching introductory creative coding in non-traditional educational contexts. More soon.
