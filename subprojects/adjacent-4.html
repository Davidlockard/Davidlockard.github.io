Issue 4 was published October 2018. It is our first issue with a theme - Bodies and Borders - and it features articles by Lee-Sean Huang, Ziyu He, Gil Naamati, Katya Rozanova, and others.

This one is my last issue as managing editor, and I am so pleased to pass the reins forward to Carrie Wang and Alden Jones!
