Responsive NYU window display


Together with the talented Itay Niv and Hayeon Hwang, I designed and constructed a window display advertising ITP's journal Adjacent.


By pressing their hand to the window, pedestrians passing 8th street and Broadway set in motion a marble run that flicks channels on a TV monitor.

The installation stood in place for about three months, and was operated thousands of times.





One interesting challenge of this project was the elevator for the ball. This problem was eventuall solved via a pretty intricate little magnetic elevator chute which I am very proud of.
