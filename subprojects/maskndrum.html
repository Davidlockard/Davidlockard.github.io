Performance with Mask and Drum


Excerpts from a 5-minute performance, created with percussionist Roy Ben Yosef, and shown at the annual New Interfaces for Musical Instruments show, at Littlefield NYC. Six hinged potentiometers manipulated through the mask's 'flaps' send a continuous flow of data to a music-generating program made on Max.
