Adjacent - Issue 2


itp.nyu.edu/adjacent/issue-2/


Our second issue was published March 2018. It features articles by Susan Jacobson, Sannitti Pimpley, Leonardo Solaas, Mimi Yin, Jen Kagan, Julia Irwin, Marina Zurkow, Anastasis Germanidis, Sejo Vega Cebrián, Carrie Sijia Wang, and Stephanie Koltun.

It also features original art by Alejandro Matamala, Jina Jung, Katya Rozanova, Hadar Ben-Tzur, Hye Ryeong Shin, and Camilla Padgitt-Coles.
