Translation


In the past I have translated texts from Hebrew to English: for online publications such as Haaretz and +972, as well as for individuals and NGOs.

One major job I am proud of is translating testimonies for the Israeli human-rights NGO Breaking the Silence. All of these can be read in their English website, or in the book This is How we Fought in Gaza - Soldiers' testimonies and photographs from Operation "Protective Edge" (2014)
